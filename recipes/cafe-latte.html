Cafe Latte Recipe

Description

There's nothing in this world better than Cafe Latte to start your day in the morning, am I right

Ingredients and Tools

For the best cup of coffee, there's nothing better than freshly grinded and roasted coffee beans.

  • Tools
    • Electric coffee beans grinder
    • Manual or electric espresso maker
    • Milk steamer
    • Coffee tamper
  • Ingredients
    • Fresh coffee beans
    • Fresh plain milk (non-UHT)
    • Water

Steps

This one is difficult but with practice it's going to be much better and definitely worth it!

  1. Grind those beans to espresso level, not too powdery tho
  2. Put the grinded coffee in the portafilter and tamp it real good
  3. Put the portafilter in the espresso maker and either pull the lever or press the button depending on your espresso maker. Aim for 9 bar pressure. Rest the espresso while you steam the milk
  4. Make sure when you steam foam is created and not too hot
  5. Mix the milk with the espresso, and voila your morning drink is ready!
