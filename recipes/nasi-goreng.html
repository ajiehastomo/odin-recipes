Nasi Goreng Recipe

Description

This one is a legendary recipe from Indonesia, y'all know this already!

Ingredients

For this one here's what y'all need!

  • Rice (duh)
  • Oil
  • Chili sauce and soy sauce
  • Eggs
  • Salt and sugar
  • White and red onion

Steps

The steps are quite tricky if you're new.

  1. Press the white and red onion real good, add a little salt and sugar while you're at it!
  2. Put oil in a pan and cook the ingredients from step 1 until it smells delicious!
  3. What are you waiting for, put the rice in the pan and mix it up real nice until it's a little brown
  4. Once the rice is cooked, remove it from the pan and fried your egg (sunny side up style)
